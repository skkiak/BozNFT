About BozNFT

BozNFT is the first Kyrgyz platform where traditional items like shyrdak, tebetey, and other symbols of our culture become digital NFTs.

We unite the art of our masters with blockchain technology to preserve Kyrgyzstan's cultural heritage and showcase it to the world.
